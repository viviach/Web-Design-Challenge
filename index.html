Summary: Weather Analytics


To create a representative model of weather across world cities, I retrieve the weather information of 500+ cities across the world of varying distance from the equator. I used Python libraries (mainly Pandas, JSON and Matplotlib) and the OpenWeatherMap API, to accomplish this.

For this model, I plot the 500+ cities latitude against some variables to evaluate how cities weather conditions vary when they are closer or farther from the equator:

  • Max Temperature (F) vs. Latitude
  • Humidity (%) vs. Latitude
  • Cloudiness (%) vs. Latitude
  • Wind Speed (mph) vs. Latitude

Visualizations per Hemisphere: Northern and Southern
